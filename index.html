Notre Histoire · Our Story

Lagos deserves
a table this beautiful.

Jardin Délices is Lagos's first French-inspired botanical dining destination — where garden aesthetics, French bistro cuisine, and the warmth of West African hospitality come together at one unforgettable address in Victoria Island.

Every detail — from the sage millwork to the peony murals — is designed to make you stay longer, photograph more, and return again.
